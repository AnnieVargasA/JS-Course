JavaScript Fundamentals – Part 1

JS is a high-level, object-oriented, multiparadigm programming language.

1. HIGH-LEVEL: Is considered high-level because it is closer to human languages and further from machine languages.

3. MULTIPARADIGM: Flexible and versatile.

2. OBJECT-ORIENTED: Relies on the concept of objects to store most types of data.

4. PROGRAMMING LANGUAGE: Lets the instruct the computer to do something.
